Did you know that mixed breeds rescued from the street can make great pets? Mixed breeds are generally healthier and more resistant because they do not carry the risk of genetic conditions that some breeds carry, so veterinary costs will be lower and the enjoyment with your pet will be complete. With our Adoption program, you will receive full support in the process of finding a pet that is made just for you, and you for it too!
